M&M Crafts

Based in Ogdensburg, NY

Seasonal ceramics, fleece blankets, and crochet

With over 40 years of crafting experience and serving the North Country as a vendor, M&M Crafts, ran by Michele Morrison has decor for every season and holiday!!

Michele Morrison is the owner of M&M Crafts.

She has lived in the North Country her whole life and has played a big role in the crafting/vendor community. She participates on the Seaway Valley Festival Board and organizes 2-3 shows per year.
