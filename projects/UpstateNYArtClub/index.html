Upstate NY Art Club

We are a collection of artists from upstate NY. Our goal is to inspire, connect, and collaborate with each other. Mediums may include DIY recycled crafts, books, sculpture, digital, and traditional art. This website also features an e-commerce feature where artists have the opportunity to sell their work.
