About TJ' Freight House

Welcome to TJ's Freight House, a unique restaurant that combines delicious dining with a passion for trains. Located in the heart of our town, we pride ourselves on offering a memorable experience for train enthusiasts and food lovers alike.

At TJ's Freight House, we believe in the power of trains to bring people together. Our restaurant is designed to reflect the rich history and nostalgia associated with trains, creating an inviting atmosphere for our guests.

Our talented chef and owner, Caitie prepares a diverse menu that caters to all tastes. From mouthwatering burgers and steaks to fresh salads and delectable desserts, our culinary creations are made with the finest ingredients sourced locally.
